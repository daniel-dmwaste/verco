How it works
1
Search your address
Enter your property address to check eligibility and view your annual allocation.
2
Select services & date
Choose your waste types and pick an available collection date.
3
Confirm & pay if needed
Free services go straight through. Extra services are charged via Stripe.
4
Place items out by 7am
We'll remind you. Items must be on the verge by 7am on collection day.
5
We collect & process
Your waste is collected and responsibly processed. You'll get a completion notification.
